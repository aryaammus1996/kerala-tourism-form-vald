PLACE TO VISIT IN PATHANAMTHITTA


ADAVI ECO TOURISM



Adavi is a major tourist destination in Konni,Kerala situated in the banks of Kallar river. This is a nice destination mainly for family trips.The main attraction is bowl boat riding.

KONNI AANAKOODU



Konni Aanakoodu located in Konni arund 10kms from Pthanamthitta town.It is a well known training center for elephants in Kerala.it is a part of Konni eco tourim.The huge elephant cages are built with strong woods.

SABARIMALA SREE DHARMASASTHA TEMPLE



Sabarimala, one of the most famous pilgrim centres in India,is situated on the mountain ranges of the western ghats and is 72km from Pathanamthitta.More than 30 million pilgrims visit the temple in Sabarimala annually,making it the largest in the world.

ARANMULA



Aranmula is a temple town.It is known as the cultural capital of Pathanamthitta.Aranmula is well known for its ancient temple dedicated to lord Krishna also known as Parthasaradhy.Here among many attractions like river Pampa,Aranmula boatrace and aranmula kannadi.
